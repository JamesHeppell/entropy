Aim

Entropy consists of two players, order and chaos. The aim of order is to score as many points as possible, and the aim of chaos is to stop order from scoring points. Ideally play two games one as chaos and one as order and the highest order score wins.

Components

There are 49 peices in total, 7 lots of 7 different colours.

Game Play

The two players chaos and order take it in turns to add pieces to the board and move them around until the board is full. Each turn consists of the following:

  • Chaos places a piece onto the board in an empty position
  • Order can then move any piece on the board, horizontally or vertically without hoping over any pieces
  • Repeat the two steps above until the board is full

Scoring

Order scores points for each line of symmetry in each row and column, based on the length of the symmetry. For example:

  • "RR" scores 2 points
  • "RGR" scores 3 points
  • "RGGR" scores 2+4 = 6 points
  • "RRR" scores 2+2+3 = 7 points
  • "RGRGR" scores 3+3+3+5 = 14 points
  • "YRGGGRY" scores 7+5+3+2+2 = 19 points
